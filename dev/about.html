About Hickory

What We Do

Hickory focuses on the bare minimum. We learn about your business and work with you to determine what will make the most impact. Then we get rid of all the extras, keeping your costs low and your focus on the important things. Depending on your needs, here are some services that Hickory might offer:

  • Website Development
  • E-Commerce
  • Social Media
  • Graphic Design
  • Branding
  • Support Program

Our Support Program is our crown jewel. This is our way of partnering with you after the projects are completed. We don't just pass the product off to you and call it quits like other agencies do. Through maintenance plans and in-person training sessions, Hickory will always have your back.

Why We Do It

Hickory Town Workshop was founded in the spring of 2015 to fill a void in the Lancaster community. There were plenty of web development and digital marketing outfits in the area, but they all focused on niche markets or well established businesses. That meant prices were too high for your small business. You could take the hit or try to manage everything yourself. But neither are great options.

We aim to change that. Our goal is to partner with small, community-focused businesses and organizations to cultivate your online presence. No frills. No extras. Only the essentials.
